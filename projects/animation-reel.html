Technical Character Animation Reel

Fall 2021
Tools: Autodesk Maya 2022, Arnold

Created for 60-220: Technical Character Animation at CMU.
Learned and utilized the 12 principles of animation, such as squash and stretch, timing and spacing, arcs, and more.

Rigs Used:

  • Body Mechanics II: Ultimate Bony v1.0.4
  • Body Mechanics: Ultimate Bony v1.0.4
  • Style Walk: Ultimate Bony v1.0.4
  • Vanilla Walk Cycle: BuckID v2.1
  • Ball and Tail: CMU Squid Rig 2.0
  • Paddle and Tail: CMU Pendulum Rig 1.0
  • Ball Bounce II: CMU Ball Rig 1.0
  • Ball Bounce: CMU Ball Rig 1.0
